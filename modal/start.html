Welcome to Sequence Alignment Teacher

  • Welcome to the Needleman-Wunsch sequence alignment teaching tool.
  • Sequence alignment in the field of Bioinformatics is a computational process for deriving a quantitative similarity between organic sequences. These sequences can include chains of amino acids or nucleotides that make up DNA.
  • Begin by entering two sequences into the input boxes at the top of the page and clicking the run button. You may set various parameters for the computation on the left panel.
  • Click on any of the blue question mark icons to learn more about the component being utilized in the process.
  • Visit the FAQ page linked in the top navbar to get more information.
