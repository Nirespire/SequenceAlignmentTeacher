Alignment Methods

Global

  • aligns the entire sequence; align all positions in sequence 1 with sequence 2, there will be mismatches, suitable for similar sequences

Local

  • align 2 subsequences; align substrings of each sequence instead of the whole sequence to find highly similar regions

Pattern

  • align a complete sequence to a subsequence of another sequence

Dovetail

  • align opposite ends of sequences; aligns prefix of one sequence with the suffix other sequence
